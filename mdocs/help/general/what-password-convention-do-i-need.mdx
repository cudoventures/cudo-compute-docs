---
title: What password convention do I need?
category: General
updated: 2025-02-24
status: published
---

If you are setting a root or admin password for your virtual machine, this must follow the below convention:
- Have between 6 and 64 characters
- Have one upper case letter
- Have one lower case letter
- Have one special symbol Example: !@#$%^&*
- Have one digit

If you are setting a password for your account, this must follow the below convention:
* Have at least 8 characters
* All Unicode and ASCII characters are aloud
* The email address must not be present on this list of compromised emails.
