---
title: I forgot my password, what can I do?
category: Accounts
updated: 2024-10-22
status: published
---

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

1. Use the "Forgot you password?" button from the sign-in page.
2. Type in the email address used to create the account and submit your request.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset email.

Please reach out to our support team if you are still experiencing issues with recovering access to your account.
