title: What payment methods do you support?
category: Billing
updated: 2025-05-29

---
We support the following payment methods:
- Bank cards, both credit and debit, such as Visa or Mastercard.
- PayPal 

If you are processing large payments, please [open a support ticket](https://compute.cudo.org/?support=open) to discuss alternative payment methods.
