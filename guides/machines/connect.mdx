## SSH onto a bare metal machine

To SSH onto a bare metal machine, you can use the "SSH" item from the options menu on the [bare metal machines list page](/guides/machines/manage-a-bare-metal-machine). Or you can use the quick buttons at the top right-hand side of the bare metal machine details page.

<Note>
Learn how to generate SSH keys & add them to your bare metal machine in our [SSH key tutorial](/tutorials/how-to-generate-ssh-keys)
</Note>
