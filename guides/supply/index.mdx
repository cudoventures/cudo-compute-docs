## About CUDO Compute
Over the past few years, we have been developing a distributed computing marketplace, that will utilize available computing and bandwidth resources for two consumer audiences; High performance and low latency edge computing. We are seeking new partners to monetise your available resources, providing new revenue streams to your business.


## Become a supplier
We are always on the lookout for new bare metal suppliers for the CUDO Compute platform. This is to ensure we can achieve the scale and performance that our customers are looking for and allow our partners to monetise their available resources and provide new revenue streams to their business.

We are currently focusing on targeting data center vendors and service providers, however if you meet the [requirements](/guides/supply/requirements) for a supplier, please reach out via our [website](https://www.cudocompute.com/) and register your interest.
