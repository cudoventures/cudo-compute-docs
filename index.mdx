## Why CUDO Compute?
CUDO Compute is a democratised cloud platform, providing unified access to cloud resources in privately operated data centers around the world.

Our platform allows you to easily deploy virtual machines, whether that be programmatically with our [CLI tool](cli), [REST API](api) or through our [web dashboard](https://compute.cudo.org).
